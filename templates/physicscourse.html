Core Principles

Physics is built on several fundamental concepts:

  • Conservation Laws: Energy, momentum, charge
  • Newton's Laws: Motion and gravitation
  • Wave-Particle Duality: Quantum behavior
  • Field Theory: Forces at a distance
  • Relativity: Space-time relationships

Branches of Physics

Major areas of physical study:

  1. Classical Mechanics: Motion of macroscopic objects
  2. Thermodynamics: Heat and energy transfer
  3. Electromagnetism: Electric and magnetic phenomena
  4. Quantum Mechanics: Subatomic particles
  5. Astrophysics: Celestial objects and cosmology

Scientific Method

Physics approach to investigation:

  • Observation: Natural phenomena
  • Hypothesis: Testable explanation
  • Experimentation: Controlled testing
  • Mathematical Modeling: Quantitative predictions
  • Theory Development: Comprehensive frameworks
