Core Concepts

Digital marketing encompasses several key disciplines:

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Organic visibility strategies
  • Pay-Per-Click (PPC): Paid advertising models
  • Content Marketing: Value-driven content creation
  • Social Media Marketing: Platform-specific engagement
  • Email Marketing: Direct customer communication

The Digital Marketing Funnel

Customer journey from awareness to conversion:

  1. Awareness: Top-of-funnel content and ads
  2. Consideration: Middle-funnel engagement
  3. Conversion: Bottom-funnel actions
  4. Retention: Post-purchase engagement
  5. Advocacy: Customer referrals

Key Strategic Challenges

Critical issues in digital marketing:

  • Attention Economy: Capturing user focus
  • Data Privacy: GDPR and regulations
  • Platform Algorithms: Changing visibility rules
  • ROI Measurement: Proving campaign effectiveness
  • Omnichannel Integration: Consistent cross-platform experiences
